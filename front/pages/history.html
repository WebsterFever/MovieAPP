History of Cinema

Cinema began at the end of the 19th century and has evolved from silent black-and-white films to today’s high-budget digital productions.

From early pioneers like the Lumière brothers to modern CGI-heavy blockbusters, cinema has continuously reinvented itself through innovation and storytelling.

Today, it remains one of the most powerful forms of global entertainment and artistic expression.
